The Goal

Output for one game turn
One line: the action of your agent that can be one of these:
During the PUSH turn:
  • PUSH id direction where id can be 1, 3 or 5 and direction UP, DOWN, LEFT or RIGHT
  • Example: PUSH 3 UP
During the MOVE turn:
  • MOVE amount direction where amount can be 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 or 6 and direction UP, DOWN, LEFT or RIGHT
  • PASS
  • Example: MOVE 2 LEFT or PASS
